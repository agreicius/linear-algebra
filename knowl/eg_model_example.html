Example 1.2.9. Row echelon form.
We use Gaussian elimination to reduce the linear system
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rcc} -2x_3+7x_5\amp =\amp 12 \\ 2x_1+4x_2-10x_3+6x_4+12x_5\amp = \amp 28 \\ 2x_1+4x_2-5x_3+6x_4-5x_5\amp =\amp -1 \end{array} \end{equation*}
to a system in row echelon form.
First we apply Gaussian elimination to the augmented matrix associated to our system.
\begin{align*} \begin{bmatrix}0\amp 0\amp -2\amp 0\amp 7\amp 12\\ 2\amp 4\amp -10\amp 6\amp 12\amp 28\\ 2\amp 4\amp -5\amp 6\amp -5\amp -1 \end{bmatrix} \amp \xrightarrow[\hspace{35pt}]{r_1\leftrightarrow r_2} \begin{bmatrix}2\amp 4\amp -10\amp 6\amp 12\amp 28\\ 0\amp 0\amp -2\amp 0\amp 7\amp 12\\ 2\amp 4\amp -5\amp 6\amp -5\amp -1 \end{bmatrix}\\ \amp \xrightarrow[\hspace{35pt}]{\frac{1}{2}r_1} \begin{bmatrix}\boxed{1}\amp 2\amp -5\amp 3\amp 6\amp 14\\ 0\amp 0\amp -2\amp 0\amp 7\amp 12\\ 2\amp 4\amp -5\amp 6\amp -5\amp -1 \end{bmatrix}\\ \amp \xrightarrow[\hspace{35pt}]{r_3-2r_1} \begin{bmatrix}\boxed{1}\amp 2\amp -5\amp 3\amp 6\amp 14\\ 0\amp 0\amp -2\amp 0\amp 7\amp 12\\ 0\amp 0\amp 5\amp 0\amp -17\amp -29 \end{bmatrix}\amp \text{ (now done with first row) }\\ \amp \xrightarrow[\hspace{35pt}]{-\frac{1}{2}r_2} \begin{bmatrix}\boxed{1}\amp 2\amp -5\amp 3\amp 6\amp 14\\ 0\amp 0\amp \boxed{1}\amp 0\amp -7/2\amp -6\\ 0\amp 0\amp 5\amp 0\amp -17\amp -29 \end{bmatrix}\\ \amp \xrightarrow[\hspace{35pt}]{r_3+(-5)r_2} \begin{bmatrix}\boxed{1}\amp 2\amp -5\amp 3\amp 6\amp 14\\ 0\amp 0\amp \boxed{1}\amp 0\amp -7/2\amp -6\\ 0\amp 0\amp 0\amp 0\amp 1/2\amp 1 \end{bmatrix}\amp \text{ (now done with 2nd row)}\\ \amp \xrightarrow[\hspace{35pt}]{2r_3} \begin{bmatrix}\boxed{1}\amp 2\amp -5\amp 3\amp 6\amp 14\\ 0\amp 0\amp \boxed{1}\amp 0\amp -7/2\amp -6\\ 0\amp 0\amp 0\amp 0\amp \boxed{1}\amp 2 \end{bmatrix}\text{.} \end{align*}
We conclude that our original system is equivalent to the system
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rcc} x_1+2x_2-5x_3+3x_4+6x_5\amp =\amp 14\\ x_3-\frac{7}{2}x_5\amp =\amp -6\\ x_5\amp =\amp 2 \end{array}\text{,} \end{equation*}
which is in row echelon form.
