Fiat 3.1.8. Tuples shall be column vectors, and vice versa.

We hereby declare that for all \(\boldx=(c_1,c_2,\dots, c_n)\in \R^n\) we have
\begin{equation*} \boldx=(c_1,c_2,\dots, c_n)=\colvec{c_1\\ c_2\\ \vdots \\ c_n}\text{.} \end{equation*}
In other words, tuples are henceforth the same as column vectors.
