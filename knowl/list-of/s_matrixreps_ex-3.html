where \(B=(x^3, x^2, x, 1)\) and \(B'=(x^2, x, 1)\text{.}\) Use the defining property (4.5) of \([T]_B^{B'}\) to determine the formula for \(T(ax^3+bx^2+cx+d)\) for an arbitrary polynomial \(ax^3+bx^2+cx+d\in P_3\text{.}\)
