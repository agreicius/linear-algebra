Corollary 0.7.4. Polynomial equality via coefficients.

Assume \(X\) is an infinite subset of \(\C\text{.}\) Let \(f\) and \(g\) be polynomials on \(X\) of the form
\begin{align*} f(x) \amp =\sum_{i=1}^\infty a_ix^i \\ g(x)\amp =\sum_{i=1}^\infty b_ix^i\text{.} \end{align*}
  1. We have \(f=g\) if and only if \(a_i=b_i\) for all \(i\text{.}\)
