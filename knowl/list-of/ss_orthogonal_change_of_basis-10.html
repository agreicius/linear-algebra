Example 5.2.15.

Let
\begin{equation*} A=\begin{amatrix}[rr]\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\amp -\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\\ \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2} \amp \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2} \end{amatrix}\text{.} \end{equation*}
Check for yourself that \(A^TA=I\text{.}\) Thus \(A\) is an orthogonal matrix.
