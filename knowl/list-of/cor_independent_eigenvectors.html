Corollary 4.5.8. Diagonalizable if distinct eigenvalues.

Let \(T\colon V\rightarrow V\) be a linear transformation, and suppose \(\dim V=n\text{.}\) If \(T\) has \(n\) distinct eigenvalues, then \(T\) is diagonalizable.
