Theorem 3.8.9. Rank-nullity for matrices.

Let \(A\) be an \(m\times n\) matrix. We have
\begin{align} n \amp= \nullity A+\rank A \tag{3.8.9}\\ \amp =\dim\NS A+\dim\CS A \tag{3.8.10}\\ \amp =\dim\NS A+\dim\RS A \text{.}\tag{3.8.11} \end{align}
