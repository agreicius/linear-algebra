Theorem 4.3.2. Change of basis for coordinate vectors.

Let \(B\) and \(B'\) be two ordered bases of the \(n\)-dimensional vector space \(V\text{.}\)

    \begin{equation*} \underset{B\rightarrow B'}{P}=[\id_V]_{B}^{B'}\text{.} \end{equation*}
