Procedure 4.1.4. Computing coordinate vectors.

Let \(B=(\boldv_1,\boldv_2,\dots, \boldv_n)\) be an ordered basis of the vector space \(V\text{.}\) Given \(\boldv\in V\text{,}\) compute the coordinate vector \([\boldv]_B\in \R^n\) by following these steps.
  1. Set up the vector equation
    \begin{equation} \boldv=c_1\boldv_1+c_2\boldv_2+\cdots +c_n\boldv_n\tag{4.3} \end{equation}
    in the unknowns \(c_1, c_2,\dots, c_n\in \R\text{.}\)
