Corollary 2.4.15. Invertibility of product equivalence.

Let \(A\) and \(B\) be \(n\times n\text{.}\) Then \(AB\) is invertible if and only if \(A\) and \(B\) are both invertible: i.e.,
\begin{equation*} A \text{ and } B \text{ invertible }\iff AB \text{ invertible} \end{equation*}
