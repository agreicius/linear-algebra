Exercise Group 4.3.5.18–19. Change of basis methods.

In each exercise a vector space \(V\) is given along with two ordered bases \(B'\) and \(B''\text{.}\)
  1. Compute \(\underset{B'\rightarrow B''}{P}\) directly using Definition 4.3.1
  2. Let \(B\) be the standard basis for \(V\text{.}\) Compute \(\underset{B'\rightarrow B''}{P}\) using formula (4.3.7) from Exercise 4.3.5.17.
18.
\(V=\R^2\text{,}\) \(B'=\left((1,1),(1,-1)\right)\text{,}\) \(B''=\left((2,1),(-1,2)\right)\)
19.
\(V=P_2\text{,}\) \(B'=(x^2+1, x+1, 1)\text{,}\) \(B''=(x^2+x+1, x^2+x, x^2)\)
