Theorem 3.3.21. Function subspaces.

Let \(I\subseteq \R\) be an interval. The sets \(C(I), C^n(I), C^\infty(I), P_n(I), P(I)\) are all subspaces of \(F(I,\R)\text{.}\) Thus we have the following chain of subspaces:
\begin{equation*} P_n(I)\subseteq P(I)\subseteq C^\infty(I)\subseteq C^n(I)\subseteq C(I)\subseteq F(I,\R)\text{.} \end{equation*}

Proof.
